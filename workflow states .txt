SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT PROJECT
WORKFLOW STATE 
1. DRAFT
Created by the student
 •Action: Student uploads their work (e.g., a project report or logbook) and completes a self-evaluation.
 •Metric: The student rates their own performance against predefined criteria like technical expertise and communication skills.
 •Permissions: Fully editable by the student; invisible to supervisors. 
2. SUBMITTED
* Action:Student timestamps submission, creating a digital audit trail for the review process.
* Metric:Can no longer be edited unless not approved by the system as it checks for completeness.
* Visible to the workplace supervisor
3. REVIEWED
* Action:Workplace supervisor adds comments or pitches in ideas as he evaluates the student's performance.
* Can approve or reject according to the standardized scorecard.
NB: If rejected the model is sent back to the student for re-editing and resubmission 
4. APPROVED 
    In this case there are two stages of approval 
a) Approval from workplace supervisor 
b) Approval from academic supervisor(lecturer)

APPROVED BY WORKPLACE 
* Workplace supervisor approves
* Routed to academic supervisor
APPROVED ACADEMIC SUPERVISOR
* Final approval where the academic supervisor gives feedback in comparison to the workplace supervisor's rating.
* Fully locked
