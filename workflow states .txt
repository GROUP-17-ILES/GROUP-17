SOFTWARE DEVELOPMENT PROJECT
WORKFLOW STATE 
1. DRAFT
* Created by the student
* Can be edited freely
* Not visible to any of the supervisors
2. SUBMITTED
* Student submits
* Can no longer be edited unless not approved 
* Visible to the workplace supervisor
3. REVIEWED
* Workplace supervisor adds comments or pitches in ideas 
* Can approve or reject basing on what is required or not 
NB: If rejected the model is sent back to the student for re-editing and resubmission 
4. APPROVED 
    In this case there are two stages of approval 
a) Approval from workplace supervisor 
b) Approval from academic supervisor(lecturer)

APPROVED BY WORKPLACE 
* Workplace supervisor approves
* Sent to academic supervisor
APPROVED ACADEMIC SUPERVISOR
* Final approval
* Fully locked
* Lecturer uses submission for evaluation
